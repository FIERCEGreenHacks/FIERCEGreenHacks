FIERCE

FIERCE is a student-run environmental organization based in Fremont, CA. Through projects, activities, service, and events, FIERCE seeks to engage the youth to work towards a sustainable future. We work on a project-by-project basis, and host large events open to all local students every few months that focus on sustainability. Our first event, the Fremont Green Summit, was a success and covered in the Tri City Voice.
